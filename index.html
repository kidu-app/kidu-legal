FAQ

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. Kidu has no accounts and no servers. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Kidu Premium → Cancel.

How do I export my data?
Open Kidu → Settings → Export — you'll get a CSV file with all your entries.
